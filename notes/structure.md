# Site structure

- Landing: title, frame, navigation, reading-time estimate, scenario spine.
- Essay: long future history by year, written from 2036, with sticky year navigation and progress bar.
- Timeline: filterable timeline by category and year.
- Forecasts: baseline calls, rationale, alternative, signals.
- Forces: analytical force map with pressure, counterforce, effect.
- Signals: watch indicators by category.
- Assumptions: faster takeoff/no terminal calamity and what is not assumed.
- Branch points: major alternatives outside main narrative.
- Sources: concise research notes and method.
